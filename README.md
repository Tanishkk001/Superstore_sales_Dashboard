# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Tableau dashboard built using the Superstore dataset to analyze sales, profit, orders, and regional performance across the United States.

---

## 🚀 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights by identifying:

- High-performing regions and states
- Most profitable product categories
- Monthly sales trends
- Impact of discounts on profitability
- Top revenue and profit-generating products

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Tableau Desktop
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization
- Business Intelligence (BI)
- Dashboard Design

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Revenue: $2.29M+
- Profit: $286K+
- Orders: 5,009
- Margin Analysis

### Interactive Visualizations
- 🗺️ Sales by State Map
- 📊 Sales by Category
- 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- 📍 Profit vs Sales by Region
- 💰 Discount vs Profit Analysis
- 🏆 Top Products by Profit
- 🎛️ Interactive Filters for Category and Region

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
- California contributed the highest overall revenue.
- The West region delivered the highest profit.
- Higher discounts generally reduced profitability.
- A small set of products contributed a significant share of total profits.
